About us

Vision

To promote the culture of innovation in the valley for employment generation by providing a ecosystem for budding idea generators and innovators by linking various institutions thus harbingering innovations in various sectors through the philosophy of RICH

Mission

  • To create and develop sustainable, globally competitive facilities that will contribute towards the accelerated economic growth of the region by identifying, exploring and developing regional competitiveness.
  • To promote a vibrant, innovative and entrepreneurial environment among institutions of the valley in particular and masses in general.
  • To assist and develop new business opportunities and ventures in specific sectors that are aligned with region's unique areas of opportunity.
  • To involve various stakeholders for creating an ecosystem of innovations.
  • To strengthen institute-industry-innovator linkages.

Objectives

  • Identify social problems of diversified nature and respond effectively to the emerging challenges and opportunities where research and innovation can help to find smart solutions.
  • To act as an institutional mechanism for providing various services including information on all aspects of idea building to budding innovators.
  • To catalyse and promote development of S&T knowledge based enterprises and promote employment opportunities in the innovative areas.
  • Operate to provide institutional support in scouting, spawning, sustaining and scaling up Grass root innovative ideas.
  • To create innovation culture in the various Institutions of the region.
  • Provide support for incubation and funding support.

Programmes

  • To organise Start-up awareness camps, Entrepreneurship & Skill Development programmes in schools, colleges, Institutions, and other places.
  • To initiate innovative student projects at various levels each year for new innovative product development by the support of local business houses.
  • To promote the concept of idea bank in institutions across the valley.
  • To facilitate creation of innovation clubs in educational institutions.
